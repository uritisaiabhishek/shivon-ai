Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Please read our policy to understand how we handle your information.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Shivon AI collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other identifiable information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.

  • To provide customer support.
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our services.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request copies of your personal information.
  • Request correction of your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@shivonai.com.
